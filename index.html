EV Charger Installation

Our Chargers
Having a home charger for your electric vehicle (EV) offers several significant benefits. First and foremost, it provides convenience, eliminating the need to rely solely on public charging stations. You can conveniently charge your EV overnight or whenever it's parked at home, ensuring a fully charged battery each morning.

Additionally, a home charger saves time by offering faster charging speeds compared to regular outlets. It also offers cost savings, as home charging is generally cheaper than using public charging stations.

Overall, a home charger enhances the practicality and efficiency of owning an EV and BHNV is here to help.
Installation Process
BHNV, your reliable EV home charger installer, offers a streamlined process for hassle-free installations. We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your needs and assess your home.

Our certified technicians then expertly install the charger, ensuring compliance with safety standards. After rigorous testing, we provide comprehensive instructions for optimal operation. Count on BHNV for a seamless transition to home charging.

Contact us today for a consultation and enjoy the convenience of powering your EV at home
Maintenance
At BHNV, we go beyond installation by offering reliable maintenance services for the EV chargers we install in your home.

Our skilled technicians are equipped to handle any maintenance or repair needs that may arise. From routine inspections to troubleshooting and component replacements, we ensure that your charger operates smoothly and efficiently.

With BHNV's maintenance services, you can have peace of mind knowing that your EV charger is well taken care of. Contact us today to learn more about our comprehensive maintenance solutions.
